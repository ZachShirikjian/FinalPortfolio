WHAT I LEARNED

  • ComboCrush- Incorporated iconography within the lettering, such as the TV for the O in Combo, and fully designed the type by hand.
  • Face Pizza- Developed a bright-colored logo reflecting the cartoony nature of the game, inspired by Nintendo's Face Raiders.
  • Train Outbreak- Explored air Photoshop brushes and contrasting colors to match the realistic, survival horror theme of the game.
  • Switchometer - Created a symmetrical logo using an original font (Vortexian), reflecting the Hot/Cold Temperature gameplay mechanic.
  • Colorpillar - Designed a logo around the limitation of using only circles and reflecting the limited color palette of the gameplay.
  • Bubble Trouble - Added white highlights to the BUBBLE lettering to incorporate texture inside of the logo.
