Perseverance
Perseverance is a quote poster featuring lyrics from the song "Mirror" by Porter Robinson. Contrast between the different weights of the Helvetica and Bodoni font families are explored. Perseverance inspires those struggling to continue forward in their lives, especially after the difficult, ongoing recovery from the height of the pandemic. Created with InDesign. One of the Accepted Works featured at the Graphic Design exhibit of VISIONS 2022, a juried student honors exhibition at Fitchburg State University.
