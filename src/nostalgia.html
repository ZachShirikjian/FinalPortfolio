Nostalgia
Nostalgia is a composition based on the word “Nostalgia”, representing a parent caterpillar nurturing their child caterpillar. Overlapped color mixtures are limited color palettes are explored. Designed for the Color Dynamics class at Fitchburg State University. Created with Illustrator.

One of the Accepted Works featured at the Graphic Design exhibit of VISIONS 2022, a juried student honors exhibition at Fitchburg State University.

Nostalgia inspired the main theme and gameplay for Colorpillar. Learn More at: zachshirikjian.itch.io/colorpillar.
