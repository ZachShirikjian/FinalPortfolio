Package Design
A selection of package design mockups for potential physical releases of my game development projects. Two were also designed for a commission requested by a Fitchburg State alumn (Dextra and Land Under Rot). A mockup of a physical Nintendo Switch release for the upcoming GoobNBalloonsDX is also featured, designed by myself. These custom game covers were created to preserve the experience and memories gained from developing these games during our time at Fitchburg State University. Designed with Photoshop and put inside spare Nintendo DS game cases.

If you would like me to design some case art for your game project, please email me at zshirikjian@gmail.com.
